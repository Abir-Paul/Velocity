What happens when you book a trial class?


You need to create an account with us in-order to enjoy a live class. When you book a free trial class a representative will call you within 6-8 hours ans you will be scheduled a demo class based on your choice.Our representative will guide you with the steps and on your chosen date you have to be ready for the trial class.

You will be provided with an id to enter the class.Keep the id with you so that you can login to the class.
